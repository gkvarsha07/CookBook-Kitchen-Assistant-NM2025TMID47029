Lamb Biryani

Indian Lamb Spicy

Ingredients

  • 500g Lamb (mutton)
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 2 Onions (sliced)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp Biryani masala
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves
  • Ghee / Oil

Instructions

  1. Marinate lamb with yogurt, chili powder, turmeric, salt, and biryani masala. Rest for 1 hour.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown. Add tomatoes & spices. Cook well.
  3. Add marinated lamb and cook until tender.
  4. Boil rice until 70% cooked. Drain water.
  5. Layer lamb masala and rice in a pot. Add fried onions, mint, and coriander.
  6. Cover tightly and cook on low flame for 20â€“25 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with raita and lemon wedges.
