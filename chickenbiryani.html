Thalappakatti Chicken Biryani

Indian Spicy Biryani

Ingredients

  • Chicken – ½ Kg
  • Seeraga samba rice – 2 cups (Approx. 400 grams, soaked 15 mins)
  • Coriander seeds – 2 tsp
  • Pepper – ½ tsp
  • Fennel seeds – ½ tsp
  • Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
  • Cinnamon stick – 6 to 8
  • Bay leaf – 1
  • Mace – 1
  • Cloves – 10
  • Cardamom – 3
  • Star anise – 1
  • Kalpasi – a small piece
  • Cashews – a few
  • Shallots – 200 grams
  • Garlic cloves – 10 to 12
  • Ginger – 2 inch
  • Coriander leaves – handful
  • Mint leaves – handful
  • Green chili – 4
  • Oil – 3 tbsp
  • Ghee – 2 tbsp
  • Grinded onion paste – prepared from shallots, garlic, ginger, herbs
  • Turmeric powder – ½ tsp
  • Chilli powder – 1 tsp
  • Curd – 4 tbsp
  • Water – 4 cups
  • Lemon juice – 1 tsp
  • Salt – as needed

Instructions

  1. Grind coriander seeds, pepper, fennel, cumin, cinnamon, bay leaf, mace, cloves, cardamom, star anise, kalpasi, and cashews into a fine spice powder.
  2. Coarsely grind shallots, garlic, ginger, coriander, mint, and 4 green chilies into a paste.
  3. Heat 3 tbsp oil + 2 tbsp ghee, sauté onion paste, add spice powder, turmeric, chili powder, 4 tbsp curd, chicken, and salt. Cook 3 mins, add 1 cup water, simmer 10 mins.
  4. Add 3 cups water, salt, 1 tsp lemon juice, mint leaves, and 2 cups soaked seeraga samba rice. Boil, cover, cook low flame 20 mins, seal with weight.
