Mutton Soup

Indian Soup Spicy

Ingredients

  • 250 g mutton (with bones, cut small)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp black pepper (crushed)
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1–2 green chilies (slit)
  • Curry leaves (few)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cups water
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil/ghee in a cooker, add cumin seeds, curry leaves, and onions. Sauté till golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and tomatoes. Cook till soft.
  3. Add mutton, turmeric, coriander powder, chili powder, and crushed pepper. Mix well.
  4. Pour 3 cups water, add salt, and pressure cook for 4–5 whistles.
  5. Simmer for 5 minutes, adjust spices, garnish with coriander.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
