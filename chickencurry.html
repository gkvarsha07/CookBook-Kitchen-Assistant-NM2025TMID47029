Chicken Curry

Indian Spicy Traditional

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast (Grained) - 3 Nos
  • Chilli Powder - 2 Tbsp
  • Coriander Powder - 1 Tbsp
  • Garam Masala - 1 Tsp
  • Cinnamon Powder - 1/2 Tsp
  • Ginger Garlic Paste - 1/2 Tsp
  • Salt - As Needed
  • Oil - 2-3 Tbsp
  • Cinnamon Stick - 2 Inch
  • Star Anise - 1 No
  • Cloves - 4-5 Nos
  • Green Cardamom - 3 Nos
  • Onion (Sliced) - 3 Nos
  • Chilli Powder - 1 Tbsp
  • Garam Masala - 1 Tsp
  • Curd (Whisked) - 250 Ml

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cinnamon stick, star anise, cloves, and cardamoms. Sauté for 30 seconds until aromatic.
  2. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and sauté for 1 minute.
  4. Add chilli powder, cinnamon powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add chicken breast pieces and cook for 5-6 minutes until lightly browned.
  6. Add whisked curd and cook on medium heat for 10-12 minutes until chicken is tender and sauce thickens.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
