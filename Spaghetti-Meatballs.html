Spaghetti & Meatballs

Italian Pasta Comfort Food

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti
  • 250 g ground beef (or mix of beef & pork)
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tbsp Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 400 g canned tomatoes (crushed)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Mix ground beef, egg, breadcrumbs, Parmesan, salt, and pepper. Shape into small meatballs.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, fry meatballs until browned. Set aside.
  3. In the same pan, sauté onion and garlic until golden. Add tomato paste and crushed tomatoes.
  4. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer 15 minutes.
  5. Return meatballs to the sauce and cook another 10 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, boil spaghetti until al dente. Drain.
  7. Serve spaghetti topped with meatballs and sauce. Garnish with parsley and Parmesan.
