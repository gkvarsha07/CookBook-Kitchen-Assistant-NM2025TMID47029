Vegetable Soup

Soups Vegetable Healthy

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 cup green beans, chopped
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup peas
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried herbs (thyme/oregano)
  • Fresh parsley/coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot. Saut√© onion, carrots & celery until softened.
  2. Add garlic and potatoes. Cook a few minutes.
  3. Pour in tomatoes and broth. Bring to boil.
  4. Reduce heat, add green beans. Simmer till potatoes are almost tender (~15-20 mins).
  5. Add corn, peas, herbs. Simmer another 5 mins.
  6. Adjust salt & pepper. Garnish and serve hot.
